Our Features

Big Data

Providing independent analytics that gives the user with complete information about all sports and e-sports events taking place on the platform. The user will be able to obtain comprehensive information on any event, professionally evaluate it and improve the efficiency of his/her analysis.

Trading

Now that we've aligned the details, it's time to get things mapped out and organized.

Algorithmic bets

Providing the user with the possibility of algorithmic bets using strategies and extended tools, as well as the possibility of automatic betting.

Copying a betting strategy

One of the key features of the platform is the ability to copy rates of other users: experts, analysts and brokers. The user can set up a deposit for which bets will be placed automatically. This opportunity opens up a unique functionality for beginners and can also serve as an investment tool.

API

A full-featured opportunity for users and developers to integrate into the platform of existing solutions or create new robots and AI-advisers. Providing an opportunity to integrate custom AI solutions with algorithmic betting technologies, which allows to create a unique automatic strategy with performance indicators.

Forecasting & Experts

Providing statistics and forecasts from experts, as well as the ability to evaluate their accuracy and use them to make bets in an automated format.

Trust deposits

Deposit management provides the client with the opportunity to give his account to another user/broker/expert, etc. with a guarantee that the deposit on the account will be used for its intended purpose and will not be stolen.

Artificial intelligence

Creating a built-in AI assistant that forecasts the outcome of events and helps the user determine the success of an event based on Big Data. With AI, the user will also be able to build and test various strategies, including for working in an automated format.

Bot Platform

A full-featured Bot platform that allows to integrate the main functionality through the bot with third-party services: chats / third-party applications, etc.
