Each Kartscription holds a mix of:


  • $KART Token:** Designed to be divisible, mergeable, and tradable.

  • Jellyfish NFTs:** These NFTs can be split, merged, traded, and found on NFT marketplaces.

  • Movescription-Format Prediction Tools:** Incorporated into Kartscription as invitational rewards, serving as tools to enrich the prediction market ecosystem.

  • SUI Token Rewards:** Kartscriptions may receive rewards from the Sui Foundation and its holders, acknowledging the community's contribution to the growth of Karma Protocol.
